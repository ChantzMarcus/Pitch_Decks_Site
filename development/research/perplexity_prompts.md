## Research Guidelines

When using these prompts:

1. **Start Broad**: Begin with general design/UX research to understand possibilities
2. **Focus on Conversion**: Prioritize prompts related to conversion optimization
3. **Validate with Data**: Look for prompts that yield quantifiable results
4. **Consider Implementation**: Balance creative ideas with technical feasibility
5. **Industry Specificity**: Focus on film industry needs and behaviors

Keep track of:
- Promising features that emerge from research
- Technical complexity assessments
- Potential impact on conversion rates
- Competitive advantages offered
- Implementation timeline estimates
